Digital Women Bootcamp

The Digital Women Bootcamp is a free, virtual, 8-week tech bootcamp designed for women especially mothers. The program is 100% virtual, donor driven and volunteer-led.

#DWBC is designed to equip Nigerian women especially mums with tech skills for inclusive tech participation, career boost and economic benefits.

We seek to raise more awareness, encourage the entry and retention of more women into tech careers by providing digital skills training to 2000 mums and women yearly and 10,000 by the year 2025.

The objectives of the program are as follows:

  • To support mums returning to career life after their nursing breaks seeking careers in tech;
  • To provide training and mentorship for career women especially mums from non-traditional tech background aspiring to be computer programmers;
  • To equip women especially mums with digital literacy skills for work/life.
